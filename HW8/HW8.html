Median Household Incomes for Families of Four vs Families with No Dependents in NC Counties

Sam Rivers

I compared the median incomes of families of 4 with families with no dependents in NC Counties to serve as a proxy for understanding family demographics. Regarding families with no dependents, there are two primary assumptions that I am willing to make. The first being the majority of those families are older and their children have left the house, meaning their career progression has largely reached its peak. The other demographic I would expect is young couples who have yet to have kids, which would skew the incomes lower. With these assumptions in mind, I am anticipating counties with older populations to have a greater ratio of family of four income to families with no dependents.
